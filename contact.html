Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery within a 5km radius. For delivery, please call us directly or order via WhatsApp.

Can I book a table in advance?

Yes, we accept table reservations. Please call us at least 2 hours in advance, especially for weekends.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes, we have an extensive vegetarian menu with more than 40 options. Check our menu page for details.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have dedicated parking space for 10 cars at the rear of the building. First 2 hours are free.

Do you cater for events?

Yes, we provide catering services for events, parties and corporate functions. Contact us for custom menus.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI, credit/debit cards, and mobile wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay.
